    About Us

    Meet Gaurav Sobti, an accomplished business head; the brainchild behind the much loved YouTube Channel - Homegram.co.in

    The idea of a creating a video streaming platform dedicated to providing unbiased real estate reviews has successfully led Gaurav to become a much admired public figure in the Gurgaon real estate industry today!

    Gaurav as a leader has been proactive in showcasing his extensive expertise across various sectors, including real estate, sports management, and environmental initiatives. With over 15 years of experience in the real estate market, he has garnered comprehensive knowledge in all aspects of this industry.

    During the extensive span of his career, he has played pivotal roles in pioneering his proficiency in forming strategic partnerships, effective team management, and successful project implementation in contemporary and eco-friendly real estate solutions.
