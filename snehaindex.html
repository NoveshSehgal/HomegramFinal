    About Me

    I am a seasoned marketing and strategy professional and a recent MBA graduate from London. I thrive in a cosmopolitan environment and bring over a decade of expertise in business development, sourcing, and design. My global exposure has honed my skills in exceptional client relationship management, effective time management, and a commitment to ethical business practices. As a leader, I skillfully manage a team of young and creative individuals, positioning them to meet the growing demands of the real estate industry.
