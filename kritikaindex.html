    About Me


    I am a seasoned marketing and strategy professional with over a decade of expertise in business development, sourcing, and design, recently graduating with an MBA from London. My career has been shaped by diverse experiences and global collaborations, reflecting my passion for thriving in cosmopolitan environments and my commitment to ethical business practices. Transitioning from a merchandising background to becoming a key player in Gurgaon's dynamic real estate scene has been transformative, allowing me to embrace challenges, adapt to new environments, and hone my skills in client relationship management and market dynamics.

    At the core of my work is a deep-seated belief in the importance of human connection, empathy, and trust. Understanding the nuanced needs of my clients goes beyond transactions, focusing on genuine care and empowering them with knowledge. Through personalized strategies, transparent communication, and effective team leadership, I have helped countless clients turn their aspirations into accomplishments, consistently exceeding their expectations. My global exposure and innovative mindset position me to meet the growing demands of the real estate industry with confidence and integrity.
